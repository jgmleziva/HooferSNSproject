Sign Up Info and Policies

  • The following page shows all the relevant information about your selected trip. Please read through all the information carefully before signing up for the trip.
  • Once you have signed up for a trip you will have 48 hours to complete payment on Venmo @HoofersSNS.
  • Failure to complete payment within this time will result in removal from the trip.
  • Please arrive at the pickup location 15 minutes early to load the car.
  • Cars will wait at a maximum of 5 minutes before departing without you.
  • Please refer to the My Trips page to see which trips you have signed up for.
  • Please contact vplocaltrips@hoofersns.org with any questions or concerns about your sign ups or if you need to cancel.
  • Please note all trips are NONREFUNDABLE.
